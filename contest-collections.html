Contest Collections
Here, we aim to compile a comprehensive database of international, national, regional, and other online astronomy and astrophysics Olympiads, since astronomy-related problems are often scattered across multiple sources. Additionally, the “Others” section includes astronomy-related problems that have appeared in physics Olympiads such as IPhO, APhO, and various Physics Brawl competitions. Happy problem solving!
